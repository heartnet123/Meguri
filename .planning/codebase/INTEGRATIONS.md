# Integrations

- **Convex**: Backend as a Service platform providing functions (queries, mutations, actions) and a reactive database.
- **Better Auth**: Authentication platform integrated directly into the Convex environment via `@convex-dev/better-auth`.
- **Google GenAI**: Used for potential AI integrations/forecasting, utilizing `@google/genai`.
- **Firebase**: Appears to be used for tooling/hosting or other functions via `firebase-tools`.
